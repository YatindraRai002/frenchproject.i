Dijon

Dijon is the capital city of the historical Burgundy region in eastern France, one of the country’s principal wine-making areas. It’s known for its traditional mustard, vineyard tours, autumn gastronomic fair and building styles ranging from Gothic to art deco.

DIJON MUSTARD

BEST RESTAURANTS FOR MUSTARD IN REIMS



CIBO:

In the heart of Dijon, CIBO, led by starred chef Angelo Ferrigno, is a tribute to modern Burgundian cuisine. The minimalist dining room contrasts with the historic building housing it. Ferrigno, known for his subtle creativity, commits to an ethical cuisine that emphasizes locally sourced products within a 200km radius.

Loiseau des Ducs

In the heart of Dijon, 'Loiseau des Ducs' offers a modern culinary experience deeply rooted in Burgundian tradition. Set in the historic Hôtel de Talmay, the restaurant beautifully blends history with gastronomy.

William Frachot

William Frachot, a talented Burgundian chef, offers a blend of creative and modern cuisine at his namesake restaurant. His international journey (England, Quebec) is mirrored in his minimalist dishes that skillfully blend international flavors with regional produce.
