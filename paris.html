Croissant


One of the most iconic foods associated with Paris is the croissant. This flaky, buttery pastry is a staple of French breakfast and is loved around the world.


Ingredients

  • 1. All-purpose flour
  • 2. Unsalted butter
  • 3. Yeast
  • 4. Milk
  • 5. Sugar
  • 6. Salt
  • 7. Water

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and warm milk to form a dough. Knead until smooth, then let it rise until doubled in size.

STEP 2

Butter Layering: Roll the dough into a rectangle, and place a block of cold butter in the center. Fold the dough over the butter to encase it.

STEP 3

Rolling and Folding: Roll the dough out and fold it several times to create layers. This process is called laminating and is crucial for the croissant’s flakiness.

STEP 4

Shaping: Cut the dough into triangles and roll them up from the base to the tip, forming a crescent shape.

STEP 5

Proofing: Let the shaped croissants rise until puffed.

STEP 6

Baking: Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown.
