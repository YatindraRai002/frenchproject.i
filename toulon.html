Toulon

Toulon is a port city on the Mediterranean coast in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region of France Known for its beautiful harbor, naval base, and rich maritime heritage, Toulon combines the charm of a traditional Provençal town with a significant military presence. The city is surrounded by picturesque hills and offers a vibrant cultural scene, bustling markets, and scenic seaside promenades.

BOILLABAISSE


Bouillabaisse is a traditional Provençal fish stew originating from the port cities of the Mediterranean, especially Marseille and Toulon. It’s known for its flavorful broth, variety of fish, and aromatic herbs and spices.


Ingredients

  • 1. Shellfish
  • 2. Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3. Leeks, onions, and fennel (sliced)
  • 4. Garlic (minced)
  • 5. Olive oil
  • 6. Saffron
  • 7. Orange zest
  • 8. Herbs (thyme, bay leaves, parsley)
  • 9. White wine
  • 10. Crusty bread and rouille sauce (a garlic mayonnaise spiced with saffron and cayenne)

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté leeks, onions, garlic, and fennel until soft.

STEP 2

Add chopped tomatoes, thyme, bay leaves, parsley, and orange zest.

STEP 3

Pour in white wine and let it reduce. Add fish stock and bring to a simmer.

STEP 4

Add firm fish and shellfish to the pot. Season with saffron, salt, and pepper. Let it cook for about 10-15 minutes until the fish is tender and cooked through.

STEP 5

Serve with crusty bread and a side of rouille sauce for dipping or spreading.
