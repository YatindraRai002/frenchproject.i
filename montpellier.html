Montpellier City

Montpellier, located in southern France, is a city rich in Mediterranean culture, history, and a thriving arts scene. Known for its sunny weather and proximity to the coast, it’s a hub for French Mediterranean cuisine and wine.

Tielle Sétoise


Ielle Sétoise is a spicy octopus pie originally from nearby Sète but popular in Montpellier. This savory pie is made with a flavorful tomato sauce, octopus, and spices, all encased in a pastry crust.


Ingredients

  • 1. Octopus
  • 2. Tomato paste
  • 3. Garlic
  • 4. Onion
  • 5. Olive oil
  • 6. Chili flakes
  • 7. Flour
  • 8. Salt and pepper

METHOD OF PREPARATION

STEP 1

Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil until golden, then add tomato paste, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.

STEP 2

Add the octopus and cook until tender.

STEP 3

Make the Pastry Dough: Combine flour, salt, and water to form a dough, then roll it out.

STEP 4

Assemble the Pie: Place the filling on the pastry, cover with another layer of dough, and seal the edges.

STEP 5

Bake: Bake in the oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 40 minutes or until the crust is golden brown. Serve warm.
