Nantes City

Nantes, located in western France along the Loire River, is known for its historical significance, vibrant art scene, and rich maritime heritage. Once a major port city, it’s now celebrated for its medieval architecture, gardens, and innovative attractions, like Les Machines de l’île.

BEURRE BLANC


Beurre Blanc is a classic butter sauce originating from the Nantes region, traditionally served with fish. It’s a creamy, tangy sauce made with butter, white wine vinegar, and shallots. Simple yet rich, it embodies the flavors of French coastal cuisine.


Ingredients

For the Beurre Blanc
  • 1. Shallots
  • 2. White Wine Vinegar
  • 3. White Wine
  • 4. Butter
  • 5. Salt and Pepper

METHOD OF PREPARATION

STEP 1

In a saucepan, simmer shallots, white wine vinegar, and white wine until reduced by half.

STEP 2

Gradually whisk in cold butter cubes over low heat to create a creamy emulsion.

STEP 3

Season with salt and pepper to taste, then strain out the shallots if desired.

STEP 4

Serve immediately over fish or seafood, like sole or salmon.
