Toulouse City

Toulouse, known as "La Ville Rose" (The Pink City) for its terracotta brick buildings, is located in southwestern France. It’s famous for its rich history, aerospace industry, and lively cultural scene. The city's architecture, riverside charm, and gastronomy reflect its unique blend of Occitan, French, and Spanish influences.

Cassoulet

Cassoulet is a hearty, slow-cooked casserole of beans, meats, and aromatic herbs. Originally a peasant dish, it has become a beloved classic across France. The traditional recipe features white beans, duck confit, pork sausages, and various meats, all cooked in a rich, flavorful broth.


Ingredients

  • 1. White Beans
  • 2. Duck Confit
  • 3. Sausages
  • 4. Pork Belly or Rind
  • 5. Vegetables and Herbs
  • 6. Stock and Tomato Paste

METHOD OF PREPARATION

STEP 1

Soak and cook the beans in water until tender, then drain.

STEP 2

Brown the pork belly, sausages, and duck confit in a skillet. Remove and set aside.

STEP 3

In a large casserole, sauté onions, carrots, and garlic. Add tomato paste and herbs.

STEP 4

Layer the beans and meats in the casserole, cover with broth, and bake at a low temperature for several hours.

STEP 5

Stir occasionally, and serve when flavors are fully melded, with a crispy top layer.
