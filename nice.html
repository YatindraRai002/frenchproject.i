Nice

Nice is a beautiful city located on the French Riviera in the southeastern region of France. Known for its Mediterranean coastline, charming Old Town (Vieux Nice), and the famous Promenade des Anglais, Nice boasts a rich cultural heritage influenced by French and Italian traditions. Its mild climate, picturesque beaches, and vibrant markets make it a beloved destination for tourists worldwide.

Salade Niçoise


Salade Niçoise is a traditional French salad that showcases the flavors of the Mediterranean. It's light, colorful, and packed with fresh ingredients that make it perfect for warm weather.


Ingredients

  • 1. Cherry tomatoes
  • 2. Green beans (blanched)
  • 3. Hard-boiled eggs
  • 4. Black olives (preferably Niçoise olives)
  • 5. Red onion
  • 6. Fresh basil leaves
  • 7. Red wine vinegar
  • 8. Dijon mustard

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Prepare the Vegetables: Boil the potatoes until tender, then let them cool and slice them. Blanch the green beans and set them aside.

STEP 2

Assemble the Salad Base: On a large serving platter or bowl, arrange a bed of lettuce or mixed greens.

STEP 3

Layer the cherry tomatoes, boiled potatoes, green beans, hard-boiled eggs (halved), black olives, and red onion slices.

STEP 4

Add Tuna and Anchovies: Place chunks of canned tuna or seared fresh tuna on top, followed by the anchovy fillets.

STEP 5

Dressing: Whisk together olive oil, Dijon mustard, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.

STEP 6

Drizzle the dressing over the salad and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
