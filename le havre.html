Le Havre

Le Havre located on the northern coast of France, is an important port city known for its unique modernist architecture, especially post-WWII reconstruction. It’s a UNESCO World Heritage site and offers coastal charm with a variety of seafood dishes.

Moules-Frites


Moules-Frites or mussels with fries is a beloved dish in Le Havre especially popular along the northern coast. The mussels are usually cooked in white wine with herbs, making for a flavorful seafood experience.


Ingredients

  • 1. Fresh mussels
  • 2. White wine
  • 3. Butter
  • 4.Shallots
  • 5. Garlic
  • 6. Salt and Pepper
  • 7. Fresh parsley

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Rinse the mussels and remove any beards. Discard any that remain open.

STEP 2

Melt butter in a large pot, add shallots and garlic, and sauté until translucent.

STEP 3

Add the white wine and mussels to the pot. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes until mussels open.

STEP 4

Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately with a side of crispy fries.
