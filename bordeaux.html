Bordeaux

Bordeaux, located in the southwest of France, is known for its wine production and beautiful 18th-century architecture. It’s a vibrant city with a rich history, recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site. Bordeaux is also a gateway to some of the finest vineyards and wine estates in the world.

Canelé


A classic vegetarian dish associated with French cuisine is Ratatouille, a flavorful vegetable stew that highlights the vibrant produce of the region.



Ingredients

  • 1.Eggplant
  • 2.Zucchini
  • 3.Bell peppers (red, yellow, or green)
  • 4. Tomatoes
  • 5. Onion
  • 6. Garlic
  • 7. Olive oil
  • 8. Fresh herbs (such as basil, thyme, or rosemary)
  • 9. Salt and pepper

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Prepare the Vegetables: Dice the eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and onion. Chop the garlic and tomatoes.

STEP 2

Sauté the Base: In a large skillet or pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, sautéing until softened.

STEP 3

Cook the Vegetables: Add the eggplant and bell peppers to the pan. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until they begin to soften. Then add the zucchini and cook for another few minutes.

STEP 4

Add Tomatoes and Herbs: Stir in the chopped tomatoes and fresh herbs. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer the mixture on low heat for about 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until all the vegetables are tender and the flavors meld.

STEP 5

Serve: Ratatouille can be served warm or at room temperature, often enjoyed as a side dish or over rice or crusty bread.
