Salmon en Papillote


Salmon en Papillote is a dish where salmon fillets are cooked in parchment paper, allowing the fish to steam in its own juices along with herbs and vegetables. This method preserves the flavors and moisture, making it a popular choice in Lyon's lighter cuisine.


Ingredients

  • 1. Salmon fillets
  • 2.Zucchini (thinly sliced)
  • 3. Carrots (julienned)
  • 4. Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 5. Fresh herbs (such as dill or thyme)
  • 6. Lemon slices
  • 7. Olive oil
  • 8. Salt and pepper

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Place a salmon fillet in the center of each parchment heart. Surround with sliced zucchini, carrots, and cherry tomatoes.

STEP 2

Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and add fresh herbs and lemon slices on top.

STEP 3

Fold the parchment over the salmon and crimp the edges to seal tightly.

STEP 4

Place the parcels on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through.

STEP 5

Carefully open the parchment at the table for a lovely presentation and serve hot.
