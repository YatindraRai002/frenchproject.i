Rennes

Rennes is the capital of Brittany, located in northwest France. Known for its medieval architecture, cobblestone streets, and vibrant culture, Rennes is a mix of history and modernity. It’s a hub for Breton culture and cuisine, especially famous for its crepes and galettes.

Galette-Saucisse


Galette-Saucisse is a popular street food in Rennes, comprising a savory buckwheat pancake (galette) wrapped around a grilled sausage. It's simple yet delicious and is a staple at markets and events across the region.


Ingredients

  • 1. Buckwheat flour
  • 2. Mustard
  • 3. Butter
  • 4. Pork sausages
  • 5. Water
  • 6.Salt

METHOD OF PREPERATION

STEP 1

Mix the buckwheat flour with water and salt until smooth. Let it rest for about an hour.

STEP 2

Heat a frying pan, add a bit of butter, and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thin. Cook for a few minutes on each side until golden brown.

STEP 3

Grill the sausages until fully cooked.

STEP 4

Place the sausage on a cooked galette, add mustard if desired, and wrap it. Serve warm.
